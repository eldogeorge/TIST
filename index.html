Welcome to Toc H

Toc H Institute of Science and Technology, established in 2002, located in the suburbs of Kochi, embellished with dynamic management, diligent and dedicated faculty, and promising cream of the student community, is today a name to reckon with among the comity of Engineering Colleges in Kerala, making giant strides in all engineering branches and management studies. Toc H, a pioneer in education, offers blended learning programs combining online learning through virtual classrooms, and world-class learning materials, ensuring holistic development, thereby serving as an active growth driver and home to a diverse community of innovators and entrepreneurs.

Toc H is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and the Government of Kerala and, accredited by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) with NBA (National Board of Accreditation) accredited programs (CE, CSE, ECE & ME) and ISO 9001:2015 Certification which ensure top-quality learning environment.
